Understanding Student Dropout Patterns in School Education

Dropout Rate in India

Student dropout remains a significant challenge in the Indian education system. Despite efforts to improve access to education, many students discontinue their studies due to various social, economic, and cultural factors.

An analysis of dropout rates across different categories, including school-wise, area-wise, gender-wise, caste-wise, and age-wise, is essential for developing targeted interventions to address this issue effectively.

By conducting comprehensive analysis and implementing focused interventions, we aim to reduce dropout rates and ensure all students have access to quality education and opportunities for personal and socioeconomic development.
